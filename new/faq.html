Q: How much will it cost to participate in HackPKU 2016?

A: HackPKU 2016 is free for ALL! You don't have to pay a dime to attend this activity. We would even cover your travel tickets.

The maximum members on a team?

A: 5.

How long is the hack?

A: It is 24 hours long,the hacking place opens from 7am to 12pm.There will be sufficient beverages and snacks for everyone!

A detailed schedule of events and locations will be posted later. All projects eligible for prizes must be completed be-tween Friday night after the kick-off and Sunday morning at the hacking location.You may hack anywhere you like, but the hacking location is highly recommended.
