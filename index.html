About Us

    In the context of hackathon, the word "hack" is used to describe how multiple technologies can be used together in a new and innovative way. Teams of up to five people spend the weekend working on innovative software and hardware solutions to real-world problems. These projects range in platform and application, including elements of web development, mobile applications, drones, and more. However, many times the most important aspect of a hackathon is the community it generates and skills that inexperienced hackers walk away with.
    Hackathon as an innovative activity for students, is becoming more and more popular in schools. And PKU, the NO.1 University in China, now provides this very chance for all to develop your own project.
    What are you waiting for? Come and join us now!

FAQs







Q: The fee of HackPKU 2016?

A: Free for ALL! You don't have to pay for a dime to attend this activity. We would even cover your travel tickets.

Q: The maximum member on a team?

A: 5.

Q: How long's the hack?

A: It's 36 hours long, From Friday(April 1st) 9pm to Sunday(April 3rd) 9am.
By the way, beverages and chocolates are sufficient for everyone!



Q: When/Where can I hack?

A: HackPKU will start in the evening of Friday, April 1st with the kick-off ceremony. There will be events planned throughout the day for those who arrive early. A detailed schedule of events and locations will be posted later. All work eligible for prizes must be completed between Friday night after the kick-off and Sunday morning at the hacking location.
You may hack anywhere you like ,but the hacking location is highly recommended.

Q: What can I win?(money e.g.)

A: It's not about winning―it's about learning and making friends that last for a lifetime (so everyone's a winner!). If you're thinking, "that's adorable...but really," then we've got you covered too. A panel of professional judges will select the best three hacks based on the following criteria: creativity, technical difficulty, polish, and usefulness. By the way, top prizes are more than 30,000RMB!
